Get CanadaPost shipping cost from your woocommerce checkout page

== Description ==

Citrino Shipping for Woocommerce provides you an easy way to get CanadaPost quotes for your customer in real time directly on the checkout page. Your customers can select the shipping method they prefer. The shipping costs are shown with the discount Citrino offers you from your personal account.

When you open an account with us you'll get access to special prices in Canadapost to ship your order.

You only need to generate your personal access key in your profile and enter them in the plugin settings. 

Create your account in [Citrino](https://maplaza.citrinocourier.com/ "Citrino"). 


After that the plugin will generate the shipping quotes automatically when your customers enter their address in the checkout page

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How much is the discount ? =

The percentage discount depends of the type of account that you have with us.

= Why my customers don't get a shipping quote ? =

First you need to check if you enter your API keys correctly in the settings. 
Your customers may have entered an incorrect address.
